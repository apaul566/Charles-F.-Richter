Charles F. Richter's Impact



Richter has made a large impact on the world as a whole. From the creation of the Richter scale, to his guidelines regarding building safety in earthquakes and the books he has published, it is no doubt that without Mr. Richter's contributions the world would have experienced much more losses of life whenever these catastrophic events happen. For example, The city government of Los Angeles removed many ornaments and cornices from municipal buildings in the 1960s as a result of Richter's awareness campaigns. After the 1971 San Fernando earthquake, the city cited Richter's warnings as important in preventing many deaths. The previous example is just one out of many. The contributions that Mr. Richter has brought to the realm of science, no doubt, will be immortalised in the history books for eternity.
